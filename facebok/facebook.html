HOW TO START SELLING ON FACEBOOK OR INSTAGRAM

Social media marketing, advertising, and selling has never been easier. Let’s get started!

1.

Create your Jimdo website or online store.
2.

Select the Facebook & Instagram option in your Jimdo Dashboard.

3.

Hit “Connect” to link your Facebook or Instagram account.

3.

Select your Facebook Business Manager account or follow the steps to create one.

3.

Choose the assets you want to connect: Facebook page, catalog, pixel, and ad accounts. Done!
